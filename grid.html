Hilsha Fish

Hilsa Fish is the number 1 tasty and nutritious fish in the world. This valuable fish is mostly found in Bangladesh. Hilsa is also called Ilish in Bangladesh. Because of its high demand, this fish is very expensive in Bangladesh.

Loitta Fish

Loitta Fish is a very tasty fish. It is also called Bombay Duck. This fish is mostly found in the Bay of Bengal. Loitta fish is very popular in Bangladesh. It is a very cheap fish. This fish is also called "Jatka" in Bangladesh.
